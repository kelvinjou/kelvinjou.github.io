FaceFlip Privacy Policy

I built this app as a free app. This Service is provided by me at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

The app itself does not collect information used to identify you, nor does it collect or store any personally identifiable information internally. All facial expression tasks are performed offline, on device

Security

I value your privacy, and thus, the app does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. This site does not include any other external links, besides the one that links to this page. The app does not collect any information on local files the user opens and would only have read-only access.

TrueDepth API

The information from Apple's TrueDepth API is only used to track the user's head and face to detect when to execute a pagination task. No information collected by the TrueDepth API leaves the user's device nor is it stored on device. This information will never be shared to third-party platforms or uploaded to the cloud. The app uses ARFaceAnchor provided by Apple for processing information produced by the TrueDepth API.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
